Transformer-based Architectures

  • PVTFormer – CT Liver Segmentation via PVT-based Encoding and Refined Decoding (ISBI 2024)
  • MDNet – Multi-Decoder Network for Abdominal CT Organs Segmentation (2024)
  • TransNetR – Transformer-based Residual Network for Polyp Segmentation with Multi-Center Out-of-Distribution Testing (MIDL 2023)
  • TransRUPNet – Improved Out-of-Distribution Generalization in Polyp Segmentation

CNN-based Architectures

  • DoubleUNet – Deep Convolutional Network for Medical Image Segmentation
  • ResUNet++ – Advanced Architecture for Medical Image Segmentation
  • ResUNet++ + CRF + TTA – Comprehensive Polyp Segmentation Study
  • ColonSegNet – Real-Time Polyp Detection, Localization and Segmentation in Colonoscopy
  • NanoNet – Real-Time Polyp Segmentation in Video Capsule Endoscopy and Colonoscopy
  • DDANet – Dual Decoder Attention Network for Automatic Polyp Segmentation
  • LightLayers – Parameter Efficient Dense and Convolutional Layers for Classification

Attention-based & Hybrid Models

  • PNS-Net – Progressively Normalized Self-Attention Network for Video Polyp Segmentation
  • UNet – U-Net Architecture for Surgical Image Segmentation (ROBUST-MIS Challenge)
