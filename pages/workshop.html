Organized Workshops & Challenges

  • MedAI: Transparency in Medical Image Segmentation
    A workshop focused on transparency, trust, and fairness in medical image segmentation, held in conjunction with MICCAI and MIDL.
  • Medico: Transparency in Medical Image Segmentation
    Organized as part of MediaEval, this challenge promoted robust and reproducible segmentation methods in GI endoscopy.
  • Medico Automatic Polyp Segmentation Challenge (2020)
    International challenge for real-time polyp segmentation, detection, and classification.
  • EndoTect Challenge (2020)
    A large-scale benchmark challenge evaluating classification, segmentation, and inference time for GI endoscopy AI.
  • EndoCV 2021 - 3rd International Endoscopy Computer Vision Challenge & Workshop
    Challenge co-organized with MICCAI 2021. Focused on endoscopic polyp detection, classification, and instrument tracking.
  • ImageCLEF Medical Tasks
    Participation and contribution to challenges like VQA-Med, MedVQA-GI for question answering and GI imaging understanding.
